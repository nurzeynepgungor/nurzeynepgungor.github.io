About me

I am a neuroscientist and currently a postdoctoral research fellow in the laboratory of Denis Pare at Rutgers University-Newark, where I also completed my doctoral thesis.

My Ph.D. work focused on the bed nucleus of the stria terminalis (BNST), a heterogeneous forebrain structure that forms reciprocal connections with the amygdala. Using rodent models and in vitro electrophysiology, I aim to shed light on the role BNST plays in the regulation of fear and anxiety.
